DC OPF Equations

The equations are: $$\begin{align} \text{min} & \sum\limits_{i}^{NG} C_i(P_i) \\ s.a & \\ \\ Pmin_i & \leq P_i \leq Pmax_i & \forall i=1,...N\\ \\ \\ B\cdot \theta &= P_i - D_i &\forall i=1,...N \\ \\ \frac{1}{x_{ij}}(\theta_i - \theta_j) & \leq P_{ij,max} &\forall i,j = 1,...,N \ \text{and} \ i \neq j \end{align}$$

Where, $\theta$ and $P_i$ are the optimization variables.

The $B$ matrix, is the admittance matrix of the system (with no resistance), and it is calculated as follows:

  • $Y_{ii}$ = $ y_i + \sum\limits_{j=1}^N y_{ij}$
  • $Y_{ij} = -y_{ij}$
Where $i\neq j$. The element $y_i$ is the admittance of linear loads connected to bus $i$ as well as the admittance-to-ground at bus $i$.
